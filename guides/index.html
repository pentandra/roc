Guides

Welcome to the roc guides! These guides are designed to take you from a beginner to an expert roc developer, being able to design research applications that effectively model your research domain and can handle the intense loads required for open research.

We’re super excited about the applications that you’re going to build with roc, and the immense value that will be created by doing research this way. This will change the world and make your research so much more useful to yourself and others.

The guides are arranged in order, starting from conceptual foundations, then proceeding to more application-specific implementation. So proceed in order for a more pragmatic approach, or skip around to what seems most interesting to you.

These guides are a work in progress, so if there is something missing, or if you find a mistake or typo, please help us out by submitting a pull request or talking to us.

Good luck!
